FAQs



What is Prizrr?

Prizrr is A New Way to Win! We believe entering to win sweepstakes should be Fun and EASY! That's right, EASY!

No calls, no surveys, no long pages of spam to scroll though. Just pick your prize and enter to win!

Prizrr makes it easy and fun to enter a variety of sweeps. With 1 click entry, you're always just a click away from piles of cash, gift cards to stores like Amazon, Walmart & Target, and hot gadgets like iPhones, iPads, and KitchenAid products.

Do people really win?

We have winners every day here at Prizrr. Winners are randomly selected through our system and 100% verified.

Winner announcements are in Prizrr emails. Make sure you're subscribed!

We know sometimes it's tough to believe it, but people really do win! Our team loves sweepstakes and we wouldn't be working here if it weren't true.

How do I know if I won?

Should you be randomly selected as a lucky winner, you will be notified via email at the email address you signed up with at Prizrr. Check your email daily!

Remember, we don't require a telephone number or mailing address when you sign up at Prizrr, so email is the only way we can notify you. Add contact@prizzr.com to your Address Book and make sure it's not going to Spam or Junk folders.

Is it possible to win more than one prize at a time?

It is absolutely possible to win more than one prize at the same time. The prize drawing is a completely automated and random process. In fact, we have members who have won on our site several times. Check out our blog post highlighting Francisco’s 5 wins on Prizrr.

If I win do I have to pay shipping costs for the prize?

No, you never pay anything to Prizrr to receive your prize!

Can prizes be transferred?

No. The actual winner must claim the prize, pay taxes (if necessary), etc., and cannot transfer the prize to another person in the event they don't want the prize.

What are my odds of winning? How many people play daily?

Great question! We're all here to win, right? The odds vary drastically every single day based on the prize value and duration. We urge all members to max their entries daily so they have the best odds of winning. We also hold regularly scheduled live streams on Facebook where we select winners at random to win prizes. You can find our next event here: Cashanovas Live Events. Be sure to Like our Facebook page to stay updated!

All you need is to login or join now.

Who can win?

Prizrr.com sweepstakes is open to legal residents of the fifty (50) United States (including District of Columbia), Guam, Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands, and other U.S. territories who are eighteen (18) years or older at the time of entry. Void outside of the fifty (50) United States, District of Columbia, Guam, Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands, and where prohibited by law.

Employees of Prizrr.com and their close family/household members are not eligible—good news for you!

How do I enter?

Visit Prizrr.com from your PC, tablet, or mobile device and click Play Now. Make sure you're logged in or join now.

Choose from one of the prizes listed. Just select one to start—you can always enter others after you're finished!

After your 1-click entry, you'll see a "Processing Entry" confirmation and then that's it! You can continue entering to win any of your favorite prizes on Prizrr. Go to the Prizes page to see all available prizes.

Plus, Prizrrbers can also enter exclusive sweepstakes from Prizrr emails. These prizes are not always available on the Prizrr.com/Prizes page, so make sure you check your email daily for email-only exclusives!

Does it cost anything to enter?

Entering is FREE! Just go to Prizrr.com and create a free account. Once you have an account you will be able to enter all of our sweepstakes for free.

How many times can I enter per day?

Each prize on Prizegrab has a different maximum allowable entries per day. Sometimes 5 entries are permitted per day, sometimes only one per day! Check out the Prizegrab Prizes page and see each prize's Official Rules.

Sometimes we offer additional-- double, triple, or more-- bonus entries through Prizrr emails or through social media like our Prizegrab Facebook page. In those cases, please check the official rules on the prize entry page.

Remember, each "day" refers to 12AM ET - 11:59PM ET.
