At Prizrr we offer sweepstakes that you can enter to win prizes!

Welcome to Prizrr. If you would like to search for giveaways or request information about our rewards, please give us a call at 800-708-0151, or email us at support@Prizrr.com. We are proud to offer a dedicated phone line that we created especially for people who may experience accessibility issues with our website. Our specially-trained staff will give your call first priority and will be happy to help provide information about our giveaways and rewards. We can also assist any hard of hearing users via email at support@Prizrr.com.

We’re Here to Help!

Ready to enter to win a giveaway?! Just call our priority hotline at 800-708-0151 between the hours of 9AM and 5PM Eastern Standard Time, Monday - Friday. We’ll be happy to talk you through your options and answer any questions that you have. If you have auditory issues or are unable to call in, our customer service team will be happy to assist you via email at support@Prizrr.com.

What is this page all about?

Prizrr is committed to accessibility and constantly strives to ensure compliance with all applicable laws and guidelines. To ensure that our services are as easy to use as possible for all of our consumers, we have created this page to highlight our key offerings and to guide you to our priority hotline (800-708-0151) or our customer service email address (support@Prizrr.com), where we can answer all of your questions. We’re always open to feedback on the accessibility of our website, so please do not hesitate to share your experience with us. We look forward to serving you.
